about

Hi, I’m Pei, a Mechatronics Engineer with a background in Mechanical Engineering and a passion for automation and precision systems. I began my career in the building design industry, where I designed medical and biomedical research facilities that balanced complex technical requirements with structured project management. My drive to make a more direct impact on innovation led me to pursue a Master’s in Mechanical Engineering, specializing in robotics and controls. Today, I work in the metrology industry, where I design, build, and refine industrial automation systems that advance accuracy and efficiency in EV battery quality control.

During my undergrad years as a machinist, I discovered the joy of creating things from the ground up. That experience sparked a lifelong passion for hands-on learning—whether it’s garment design, sewing, furniture making, woodworking, or painting, I’m always finding new ways to create and build.

Please feel free to check out Projects page for engineering work and Art page for hobbies!
